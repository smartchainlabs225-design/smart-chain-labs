Traditional MLM software runs on centralized servers — the company controls payouts and data. Blockchain MLM platforms use smart contracts that execute automatically, pay commissions instantly, and cannot be altered by anyone. This builds unbreakable trust with your members.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Smart Chain Labs.

How long does it take to build a blockchain MLM platform? +
Smart contracts can be delivered within 24-48 hours. A full platform with dApp, dashboard, and leader website takes 3-7 days depending on complexity and the plan type chosen.
Which blockchains do you support for development? +
We support BNB Chain (BSC), Polygon, Ethereum, TRON, Avalanche, Arbitrum, and Solana. For most MLM projects we recommend BNB Chain for its low transaction fees and large crypto user base in India and Southeast Asia.
Do I get the source code after delivery? +
Yes, absolutely. You receive 100% of the source code — smart contracts, frontend, and backend — after project delivery. You own your platform completely with no recurring licensing fees.
Are your smart contracts audited before deployment? +
Yes. Every smart contract we deploy goes through a security audit before mainnet deployment. You receive a written audit report that you can share publicly to build trust with your community.
What MLM plan types do you develop? +
We develop Binary, Matrix (X3/X6/any size), Unilevel, ROI/Staking, Auto Pool, and Hybrid MLM plans on blockchain. We can combine any two plans for a custom hybrid structure tailored to your business model.
Do I need technical knowledge to use the platform? +
No technical knowledge is needed at all. You just share your business idea and we handle everything — from writing the smart contract to deploying on mainnet. We also provide training on how to manage your platform after launch.
